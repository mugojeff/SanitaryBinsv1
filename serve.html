We often get questions about controls & regulations that appply to sanitary waste.In this segment we answer some of this common questions to help you understand your legal obligation as a property/business owner on sanitary waste

    • what is sanitary waste?

      Also known as hygiene waste is a product of a population that is known to be infectious.It includes

      1. Used pads/tampons
      2. Nappies/daipers
      3. Condoms
      4. Any waste containing body fluids

    • How do you store sanitary waste
      1. Sanitarybin;bins should be hands free i.e foot operated opening slightly at the top with a sliding tray not to reveal the content of the bin.They should be narrow to fit and give room to the washrooms
      2. How do you dispose sanitary waste

        Its the legal obligation of the business owner to contract a NEMA registered infectious waste handler such HerBins to collect your commercial sanitary waste.It is important to note that infectious waste handlers are different from garbge/general waste collectors
